By using armor items, you can strengthen your defenses and assist with this. A number of smaller Shield bars will appear above your Health bar once you put on armor. There are 25 additional hit points in each of these shield bars. In our Apex Legends Armor & Gear guide, we go into detail on the various types and qualities of equipment, but generally speaking: White Armor will supply two Shield bars (50 HP), Blue Armor will provide three (75 HP), and Purple and Gold Armor will provide four (100 HP). With 125 HP, the new Evo Shield offers the most potential defense, but you must build up the charge for it throughout the course of a game by dealing damage.

Shields normally suffer damage before Health does, but when the Ring attacks, it deals damage to you regardless of how many shields you have and depletes your health. As we will learn later, Health and Shields work somewhat similarly, but they require different healing items to restore.
