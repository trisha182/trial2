Apex Legend's Armory Guide

Hello! and Welcome! to the Apex Legend’s Armory page. For those who are new to the game, Apex Legends is a battle royale game where you play in teams of 2s or 3s. In this game you have a good handful of different characters to select from. Each character is unique in their own way because they have their own powerful special that can help you or your teammates as you fight to stay alive in the game.

The concepts of the game:

  • • To be a team supporter
  • • Get kills and deal damage
  • • Fight to stay alive and be the last team standing
This page was created for new gamers that are new to the game Apex Legends. People that arn't new to the game are welcome as well. The point of this helpful website is to give you an opportunity to learn and understand all the weapons, heals, and armor that Apex Legend offers in the game.
